This [repo](https://github.com/LixiangHan/GANs-for-1D-Signal) contains pytorch implementations of several types of GANs, including DCGAN, WGAN and WGAN-GP, for 1-D signal. It was used to generate fake data of Raman spectra, which are typically used in Chemometrics as the fingerprints of materials.
